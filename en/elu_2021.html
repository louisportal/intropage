Your elected councillor

Louis Portal, your representative in German-speaking Switzerland

+
Contact

Originally from Bordeaux, I lived in Paris, New York, and then Zurich, where I settled in 2014 to finish my studies. Based in German-speaking Switzerland for twelve years, I founded a digital startup and spent ten years in the tech/venture capital ecosystem, with which I still collaborate, representing an international investor club for Switzerland.

Today I work in the aviation sector. I travel regularly for work, which gives me many opportunities to interact with consular representatives from around the world and stay in touch with the realities of French citizens abroad.

Committed to ASFE during the last consular elections, and in contact with many French and French-speaking associations, I was elected with the conviction that abroad, civic engagement must transcend partisan divisions and unite all French citizens. It is essential to remember that ASFE's independence is a major asset. French politics is important, but we must above all focus on the local realities of our host country. This allows us to respond effectively to your needs.
