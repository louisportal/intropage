I founded Fision in 2014, a Swiss fashion-tech startup turning smartphones into virtual fitting rooms. After six years of building the company across Zurich, Paris and New York, we exited to Zalando in 2020, one of the few Swiss fashion-tech acquisitions of its decade.

I then spent several years inside the European startup ecosystem, advising and operating across a dozen early-stage companies, covering product, go-to-market and fundraising. That work naturally pulled me toward the capital side of the table, and I joined a series of international investor clubs to back the founders I most wanted to see succeed.

Today I lead the Swiss chapter of Inovexus, a global investor club that backs ambitious technology founders from pre-seed to Series A. My role is to source the best Swiss deals, open our network to founders raising here, and help our members deploy capital with conviction across the country.
