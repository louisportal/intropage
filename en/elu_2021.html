About me

‚ñº

Born in Bordeaux, I lived in Paris, New York then Zurich, where I settled in 2014 to finish my studies. Based for twelve years in German-speaking Switzerland, I founded a digital startup and spent ten years in the tech/venture capital ecosystem, with which I still collaborate, representing an international investor club for Switzerland.

Now in the aviation sector, I travel regularly for work, providing opportunities to exchange with consular representatives worldwide and stay in touch with the realities facing French citizens abroad.

Committed to ASFE during the last consular elections, and in contact with numerous French and Francophone associations, I was elected with the conviction that abroad, civic engagement must transcend partisan divides and unite all French citizens. It is essential to remember that ASFE's independence is a major asset. French politics matters, but we must above all focus on the local realities of our host country. This allows us to respond effectively to your needs.
