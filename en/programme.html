Our Programme

Our priorities for French citizens in German-speaking Switzerland and Liechtenstein

ASFE

Defend, Support and Accompany French Citizens Abroad - ASFE is the only party whose sole objective is to protect the interests of French citizens living outside France.

1

Inform & Defend

Defend the rights and interests of the community through ASFE's action and by disseminating reliable, high-quality information on issues concerning French citizens abroad, particularly in our constituency.

2

Connect

Foster integration in Switzerland and strengthen the bond with France through networking, exchanges, and local initiatives.

3

Support

Help those in need and offer concrete solutions for mutual aid and solidarity.

4

Francophone Culture

Promote the French language and support the Francophone associative and cultural network.

5

Economic Development

Develop economic potential by facilitating exchanges and connections between entrepreneurs, talents, investors, chambers of commerce, and economic stakeholders.
